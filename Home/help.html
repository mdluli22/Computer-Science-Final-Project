Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a flight?

To book a flight, go to the Home page, enter your travel details into the search bar, select your flight, and follow the prompts to complete your booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your flight up to 24 hours before departure for a full refund. After that, cancellation fees may apply. For more details, visit our Cancellation Policy page.

How can I change my flight?

To change your flight, log in to your account and go to your bookings. Select the flight you wish to change and follow the instructions. Additional fees may apply depending on the changes made.

What should I do if I have lost my baggage?

If you have lost your baggage, report it immediately to our Lost & Found department at the airport. You can also contact our customer service for assistance.

How can I contact customer service?

You can contact our customer service team via phone at 1-800-123-4567 or email at support@syntaxonair.com. Our team is available 24/7 to assist you with any inquiries.
